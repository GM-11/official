E-CELL DTU

Fostering The Spirit of Entrepreneurship

E-cell DTU, for the past 12 years has been advocating the spirit of being a ‘creator’ by providing the students the right guidance and mentorship from venture capitalists, angel investors, and top industrialists and striving to create an entrepreneurial ecosystem. We attempt to foster new values amongst young individuals to make them an exemplary engineer imbibed with the idea of productive, innovative and independent thinking. We aim to create a 'change' with out of the box ideas and training individuals to make them the creators of this change.

ESUMMIT'20

E’summit along with its highly exhaustive and informative entrepreneurship sessions that often include top speakers from various walks of life and professional communities also organizes various fun informal events such as a standup session, an improv session, a YouTuber meet and greet as well as a band night. This all in one confluence would not only provide you with valuable knowledge and experience but can also act as a retreat from your professional life.

Startup Weekend 2019!
know more

DAY 1

Startup Weekend 2019 resuscitated with the participants full of vigor and their exciting new ideas, ready to lay a foundation to their drams – to build their own company and that too within 54 hours! Young entrepreneurs pitched their ideas. And the best made it to the top 12 resonating with the vibe of "No Talk, All Action."

DAY 2

Mentors including Deepshikha Anand, Sameer Mathur, Arindam Nath, Nikhil Gupta, Charru Khanna and Varija Bajaj carefully analyzed the idea and provided the teams with a 'Success Matra' and insights on how to deal with the business competition and reach on top of their game.

DAY 3

The final pitch was presented before the esteemed panel of judges who were Mr.Amit Gupta (VP mastercard), Mr. Gaurav Malhotra (founder ann patra), Mr.Sunil Solanki (Country Head SAP), Mr.Neeraj Singh (Director KPMG) and Mr. Rajeev Sikka (CEO Adani Gas)
