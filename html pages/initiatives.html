OUR INITIATIVES

The comprehensive idea behind DTU Connect is to provide a one-on-one mentorship from people proficient in their respective fields, and to guide the students having an entrepreneurial idea but didn't have the required means to reach out before. With this app, students have an opportunity to submit their required ideas which can potentially be converted into a product. This ensures not only exceptional innovations and brainstorming of ideas but also more job creators than job seekers.

InternDTU is a college-specific internship portal where companies can hire interns according to their needs. An Exclusive Internship Portal for students of Delhi Technological University formerly Delhi College of Engineering by Entrepreneurship Cell, it was launched in 2018 to provide quality internships to the students of DTU. After more than 2 years in the running we are happy to inform you that the platform has very well served the basic problem of getting internships with quality exposure and in a wide variety of fields. InternDTU has also successfully helped students from numerous backgrounds in honing their skills according to the marketâ€™s demand.

Ecell, DTU actively organises numerous workshops and provides an exceptional platform for the students to expand their networking, confidence in a professional environment where students can indulge themselves in self-assessment and critical thinking.
Speakers from all across the country who have done exceptionally well in their respective fields provide valuable insights to the students and helped them to clarify their career path and career related dilemmas.
One of the eminent speakers was Mr. Tarun Dholakia. He is a 99 percentile score in GMAT and runs his own institution Floating GMAT to assist aspirants to reach greater heights.

QuarINTERN an initiative of E-CELL DTU aimed to bestow individuals an edge over their peers by helping them utilise the desolate time period due to the COVID lockdown by providing them with quality "work from home" internships.
The initiative was able to attract more than 20 startups to collaborate and offer work from home internships to students of various colleges in Delhi. More than 100 internship opportunities (both technical as well as non-technical) with profiles varying from Web Development to Marketing to Sales were offered to the students with stipends as high as 30k.
