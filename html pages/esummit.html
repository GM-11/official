Why Attend

Eloquent Orators

An astounding opportunity to listen, meet, interact and connect with the ones who made an impact with glorious achievements in their spectrum of work. Attend a star-studded Comedy Evening with the best Standup Comics and Youtube Entrepreneurs across the country.

Competitive Challenges

Who doesn't like to end a competitive challenge by lifting the winning trophy? An astounding line-up of competitions including Business Plan Competition and overnight Business Hackathon waiting for you at E-Summit'20 with numerous rewards to be won. These one of a kind bewildering competitions present our budding entrepreneurs with enormous exposure and learning.

Speedy Internships

Get a golden opportunity to sit in for speedy interviews and grab yourself an internship or placement in the well-established startups across the country at Startup Internship Fair. E-Summit'20 presents more than 50 startups from a plethora of diverse domains to chose from in non-arguably the largest internship fair of North India.
